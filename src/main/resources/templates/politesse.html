Intitulé

Liposem is a unique proprietary herbal formulation with antioxidant and hypolipidaemic properties. It contains 17 active herbal ingredients like Vrikshamla (Garcinia indica), Guggulu (Balsamodendron mukul), Saptaranga (Salacia sp.), Arjuna (Terminalia arjuna), Salmali (Bombax malabaricum), Madhuka (Madhuca indica) etc. It could help lower the atherogenic index and reduce body weight (obesity) when combined with a balanced diet and regular exercise. Liposem aids in curbing hyperlipidaemia to a certain level. Liposem could therapeutically minimize vascular initial damage and diet-induced hyperlipidaemia.It works on reducing LDL and Triglycerides and increasing HDL, which is helpful in reducing the risks of cardiovascular diseases. This herbal tablet may naturally act upon artherosclerosis by renormalizing the imbalanced lipid profiles and may help in offering key cardio-protection. Liposem™ is the Doctor’s ideal choice for cholesterol management, Naturally!
